About Team AsimovCore

The Asimov Core team was established in December 2021 within the Asimov Technology Club. Team purpose; To produce projects by laying the groundwork for the preparatory class and first year student members on robotic coding, artificial intelligence, mechanics, software and electronics.

Works Of The AsimovCore Team

Asimov Technology Club has the authority to contact with all teams within its body and can take an active role in club activities. Asimov Core team is the team that includes members who will take an active role in the operation of the club in the coming years and lay the foundations for the establishment of new teams.
