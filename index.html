What is Asimov Technology Club?


Asimov Technology Club, which was established on November 9, 2020 within the body of Istanbul Kultur University, is a student club that produces projects based on engineering and aims to participate in national and international competitions with the projects it produces. Another aim of our club is to create a student movement at Istanbul Kultur University. One and a half years after its establishment, it has successfully completed its first steps as a large community with more than 50 active members. It is aimed to ensure the continuity of the club with our core teams formed as well as our members working with 4 major projects. Our projects consist of Asimov-X Team of Rockets (Model Rocketry), Aysar UAV Team (Fixed Wing Unmanned Aerial Vehicle), HelloWorld Team (Software Development Team) and IKU Autonomous Teams (Electric Autonomous Car).

  • As a result of these projects, it is progressing with the aim of becoming a club that builds bridges between companies and students in the sector, together with students who produce and learn while doing.

Why Asimov?

At the very beginning of our adventure, we were planning to establish a club whose name we dreamed of being the 'Technology Club', whose goal was to advance in the topics of technology and to produce projects at the same time. Afterwards, we exchanged ideas with our friends about the mission and symbol of our club, considering it as our road map that the first priority in new formations is to set a mission. We included Asimov's name in our club because biochemist and writer Isaac Asimov reflected the future technology in his books in his science fiction works in the 1970s and we will be a technology-based club. When we see that our slogan “Dream More Do More!” is combined with the name Asimov, inspired by Isaac, we carry out our work with our team, multiplying our excitement and desire.
