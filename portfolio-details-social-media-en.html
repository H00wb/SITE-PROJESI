About Social Media Team

The purpose of the social media accounts of the Asimov Technology Club, which was established on November 9, 2020 within the body of Istanbul Kultur University, is to inform its members about the latest technology news and events organized within the club. Our team creates their content and presents them on their social media accounts with their original designs, about the technological developments that will expand the visions and perspectives of our teams -members- participating in various technology competitions.

The Statistics of Our Social Media Team

From the first day we manage our social media accounts, our goal is to reach people who are more tech-savvy than the number of followers. The number of views of our page in the last 1 month is 6.999.
What is important for us is our interaction and organic followers. For more, always follow Asimov Technology Club on your Instagram and Linkedin accounts.
