About IKU OTONOM Team

Our IKU OTONOM Team was established in November 2021 to make unique autonomous vehicles and to make Istanbul Kultur University the leading school in the field of autonomous vehicles, and then to represent our country in this field. Our team members have been assigned to the fields they are related to for this purpose and our team has been formed in this way. Our team participated in the 2022 TEKNOFEST competition in the ROBOTAX - AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE category with the desire to achieve their goals by gaining experience and to bring new successes to our school.

The works of IKU OTONOM Team

Our team consists of 3 different teams: software, mechanics and electronics. Our software team is responsible for the vehicle's autonomous tasks and the integration of the necessary sensors. Our mechanics team is working on the chassis and shell design in an original way. Our electronics team, on the other hand, works on combining the necessary connections for the vehicle to work in the targeted way and communicating these systems together. We carry out our work in our electric car workshop in our school. We work by meeting there during our working hours and the rest of the hours when we are convenient.
