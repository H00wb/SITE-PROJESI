About Aysar UAV Team

The Aysar UAV team, which is part of the Istanbul Kultur University Asimov Technology Club, was established in March 2021. As Aysar UAV team, we set ourselves the goal of "Free and independent in our skies" and continue our work in this direction. Our team continues its activities in order to make a difference with a dynamic team and an original design by constantly improving itself in the aviation industry. Our team has scanned the national and international aviation literature in detail, and as a result, it will participate in the national-international competitions in the future after participating in the TEKNOFEST International fixed wing competition, which will be held this year, with a fixed-wing type vehicle capable of autonomous flight.

Works of Aysar UAV Team

Our team continues to work in 4 different areas: image processing, simulation, avionics and mechanics. In this process, we learn to simulate our model with ROS, to write deep learning algorithms in Python, to detect objects for the mission of the UAV, to perform durability tests of the model with Solidworks, and make improvements to get the best performance. In this study, our team will discuss how the literature corresponds to real life, what kind of changes the work done in the digital environment undergoes as it becomes tangible, what problems it encounters and how to find solutions to these problems, how pre-flight risk analysis, mechanical analysis and all structural and avionic tests are carried out and how to perform them.It aims at how project, time and risk management should be done. In addition, the members of our team had the opportunity to develop themselves in their fields, as well as the studies they received at the university. We use the following technologies while development of UAV: Gazebo, Solidworks, Mission Planner, Deep Learning, Machine Learning.
