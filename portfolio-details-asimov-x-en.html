About Team Asimov-X

Asimov-X Rocket Team reaches members who aim to design and manufacture rockets, which are the building blocks of today's and future defense industry, and to represent our country in the aviation industry. In this direction, it has participated in the 2021 and 2022 TEKNOFEST competitions, as it is one step closer to our goals and drawing a roadmap for us in our work. He participated in the TEKNOFEST Medium Altitude category in 2021 and gained experience that will carry him further. While we still carry the excitement and responsibility of the first day of our establishment, by developing his project, he applied to the competition again this year and received 647.5 / 765 points from the Preliminary Design Report and became 54th among 271 teams. We are already looking forward to the day of the flight with our team.

The Work Of The Asimov-X Rocket Team

Our team includes aerodynamic design, avionics (electronics), software and rescue system mechanics subteams. Our aerodynamic design team deals with the entire interior and exterior design process of the rocket. It also obtains the drawings and simulation results of the rocket through the programs. The avionics team continues to work on embedded system development. Avionic systems are critical for the separation of the rocket at the target altitude. The software team ensures that the data transferred from the rocket to the ground station is processed and the GPS data to be received when the rocket reaches the earth is organized and the rocket is found. In the rescue system mechanics, where we have proven our originality, our team offers the most ideal and environmentally friendly solutions for the separation of the rocket at the targeted altitude. Although we have different teams for different tasks, we continue our work by meeting at school and exchanging ideas together in our spare time.
