About Hello World Team

Hello World team within the body of Asimov Technology Club established at Istanbul Kultur University; They learn the technologies used in the development of digital software and try to spread the projects developed by using these technologies to people.

Hello World Team works as a competency and performance unit with the Information System Technologies department of Istanbul Kultur University in the PUMA project, which is planned to be implemented in the school and will make the education processes of the students more controlled and enjoyable by gamifying the education processes of the students.

We participated in the HaCXathon idea development competition organized by Turkcell in March 2022. In this event, where 80 university students competed in 20 different teams, we qualified for the 3rd place with the idea we produced and the prototype we created.

We gave an interview with Kultur College's Principal Selin TATAROĞLU in the Innovation Culture Magazine.

We developed the website of the European Union Project with the work of the Hello World Team. (atamtechnology.com)
