Who Are We?

Hello World++ Team is a team that was established in April 2022 to represent IKU ASIMOV club on the corporate side apart from competitions and festivals. As a team, our goal is to provide freelance experiences to our team members in web and mobile application development, while offering the chance to develop themselves in these fields.
